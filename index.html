About Anaya AI

Anaya AI is an AI-powered conversational assistant designed to help users through messaging platforms such as WhatsApp. Our goal is to provide helpful, accurate, and engaging conversations while respecting user privacy and maintaining responsible handling of personal information.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Anaya AI collects, uses, and protects information when you communicate with our AI assistant.


📩 Information We Collect

We may collect messages you send, your WhatsApp phone number, your display name (when available), and technical information necessary to deliver our services.

⚙️ How We Use Information

Information is processed solely to understand your requests, generate AI-powered responses, improve our services, and maintain system security.

🔒 Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

🤝 Third-Party Services

To provide AI-generated responses, messages may be securely processed through trusted third-party AI service providers. These providers process information only to deliver requested functionality.

Data Usage

  • We use message content only to generate responses requested by users.
  • We do not sell personal information.
  • We do not use conversations for advertising purposes.
  • Access to stored information is limited to authorized personnel where required for service operation.
  • Users should avoid sharing sensitive personal, financial, or medical information unless necessary.

Your Rights

Depending on your location and applicable laws, you may have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information. You may also discontinue use of the service at any time.
